About Us

Break the Cycle is a movement dedicated to ending corruption in the Philippines.
We believe real change begins when we stop the lies and start the change — demanding accountability, integrity, and transparency in every level of society.


Our organization, TAPAT: Tunay na Aksyon Para sa Anti-Korapsyon stands for honest leadership and active citizen involvement.
We work to raise awareness, empower communities, and inspire action against the systems that hold our nation back.

Our Community

The Reality of Corruption

Corruption in the Philippines drains billions of pesos every year. These are funds that should be invested in education, healthcare, infrastructure, and essential services for every Filipino. Instead, this money falls into the hands of corrupt officials while countless families continue to struggle. From rigged biddings and ghost projects to extortion and embezzlement, corruption reaches every sector of society. It deepens inequality, weakens our democracy, and erodes public trust in our institutions. But change is possible. When citizens unite, demand transparency, and hold leaders accountable, we can break the cycle of corruption and build a Philippines where every generation can thrive.

How you can help.

Change starts with you. Here are concrete ways every Filipino can join the fight against corruption.

SPEAK OUT

Use your voice on social media. Share stories of corruption and demand accountability from officials.

REPORT CORRUPTION

File complaints with the Ombudsman, COA, or anti-corruption agencies. Your report can trigger investigations.

KNOW YOUR RIGHTS

Educate yourself about transparency laws, FOIA requests, and citizens' rights to information

EDUCATE OTHERS

Share information with family and friends. Host community discussions about integrity and accountability.

JOIN THE MOVEMENT

Connect with local anti-corruption groups and civil society organizations fighting for change.

AMPLIFY THE MESSAGE

Share this campaign using #BreakTheCyclePH. Every share raises awareness and builds momentum
